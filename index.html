Mission

We aim at addressing issues of unsustainaible practices that threaten the integrity of the unique Korup Rain forest and its ecosystems through education and improvement of economic and social well-being of the local inhabitants.

group

Community

We work with Korup National Park villages targeting especially the youths having in mind that they are the future of Korup National Park's biodiversity.

forest

Vision

Our aim is that by the year 2030, the Korup National Park will have thesame bounds on ground as it is on map, with increased levels of wildlife species and improved livelihoods for the local inhabitants.

About Us

Nature Conservation And Rural Development Initiative is a registered member of council of international fellowship (CIF) / United States of America (CIPUSA) it’s also a registered member of the South – West Civil Society organizations Network (SWECSON) and the Ndian Division Civil Society Organizations Network (NDICSON). Nature Conservation and Rural Development Initiative also works in collaboration with Korup Rainforest Conservation Society (KRCS) Mr. Ekp Peter, the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Nature Conservation and Rural Development Initiative was one of the founding members of the Korup Rainforest Society (KRCS) Church held the position of Senior Conservation Education Officer at the time.
